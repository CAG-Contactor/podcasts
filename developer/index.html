What is this podcast about?

"Jag är en full stackare" is a podcast delivering weekly summaries of the most relevant news in the developer world – with a focus on technologies like Java, Go, Spring Boot, Quarkus, React, Angular, and AI.

All news stories are handpicked by us at CAG Contactor to cut through the noise and deliver only what truly matters. Both the podcast script and narration are AI-generated — as part of our ongoing exploration and education in AI technologies.
